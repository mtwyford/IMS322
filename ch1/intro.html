It initially resided on a Canvas LMS site, but I modified the content to live on GitHub Pages that is open to the public, so I can send students who at looking for a self-study course to the site, or adopt it into my CS1 or CS2 Introductory courses at my high school.

JavaScript: A brief Overview

      We begin this section of the course with a brief, high level look
at Javascript and its function and role in a web page.  Though 
JavaScript has been around many years and new technology, libraries and
tools have been created to extend and enhance the JavaScript 
functionality of a site, such as Node (formerly Nodejs - a not to the
 parent scripting tool, Javascript still plays a vital role in helping
students to understand some basics of the model of how modern, yet 
simple html pages work.

 and millions of users, simple web pages can be made by using and
 understanding the analogy or model of the three legged stool.  
Each leg of thee stool represents a core technology of a web page.

    Welcome to the JavaScript 101 Mini Course

      This is an adaptation from my IMS322 course on JavaScript and Interactive Web Pages at Miami University of Oxford. It initially resided on a Canvas LMS site, but I migrated (and in some cases, recreated) the content to live on GitHub Pages that is open to the public, so I can send students who at looking for a self-study course to the site, or adopt it into my CS1 or CS2 Introductory courses at my high school.
      This is a mini course deisgned to expose learners to the foundations of JavaScript, it's place within an interactive web site, and adapts material from W3Schools.com and the lecture slides from Duckett's JavaScript & JQuery Book (11e)

    Though not all the content of the course will come from the book, it's a good starting point to discussions and projects, etc.
